ADMIN

    Here Admin can view a list of Doctors and Patients and assign Doctors to some set of Patients

    Admin can see both the Doctor and Patient queries and also generate the condition of patient

    Admin will validate the Permission and Unique ID generated at the time of raising a permission.

DOCTOR

    Doctor would be having the list of patients and checks for the condition of Patient.

    Each Doctor would be assigned to particular patient and should maintain details

PATIENT

    Patient would have Id,Name,Address and details of his condition which would be seen by Doctor

    If the pulse rate or blood pressure rate, body temperature not inline with the recommendable range should send the alert to the doctors.
